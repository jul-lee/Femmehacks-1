Habitat

Temple Squirrels, also known as The Squirrels, are infamous on the Temple main campus.

Temple's main campus lies in the heart of North Philadelphia, along Broad Street

Common sightings within the Temple Campus include: Paley Library, Bell Tower, Founder's Circle, and Beury Beach

Diet

The diet of a common squirrel include acrons, hazel nuts, berries, fungi, and even assorted plant materials.

On rare occasions, they will consume small animals, bird eggs, and nestlings

However, this diet becomes much more diverse for the special subspecies of Temple squirrels. The intake of foods expands to include Richie's, Chipotle, Halal, Crepes, and nearly anything the squirrels can get their little paws on..

Except dining hall food, because the squirrels know not to eat anything that may make them feel sick
